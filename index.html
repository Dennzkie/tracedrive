Overview

Project Concept

TraceDrive is a centralized web-based Vehicle Accident Records and Case Tracking System designed to assist authorized personnel in recording, organizing, retrieving, and monitoring vehicle accident-related information through a digital platform.

The system aims to improve accident documentation, information accessibility, record organization, and case monitoring by providing a centralized database for managing vehicle accident records and related information.

TraceDrive enables authorized personnel to:

Search accident records using vehicle plate numbers
Manage vehicle, owner, and driver information
Record and maintain accident incident information
Upload and manage supporting evidence and documents
Monitor and update accident case statuses
Search and filter accident records efficiently
Generate reports for documentation purposes
View accident statistics and summaries based on available accident records

The system will utilize vehicle accident records covering a three-year period (2023–present) to support information management, statistical reporting, and accident record monitoring.

Challenge

Problem Setting

Vehicle accident-related records are often maintained through multiple documents, spreadsheets, and paper-based files. As records increase over time, retrieving accident information, organizing documentation, and monitoring case progress may become difficult and time-consuming.

In situations where the individual driving a vehicle differs from the registered owner, identifying and documenting the actual driver involved in an accident may also become challenging without a structured recording system.

Furthermore, reviewing large volumes of accident records manually may affect the efficiency of generating reports, monitoring case developments, and producing statistical summaries needed for documentation and decision-making.

The absence of a centralized records management system may result in:

Inefficient Retrieval of Information

Time-consuming process of searching through physical files and paper-based records for specific accident-related information.

Disorganized Accident Documentation

Scattered records across multiple documents and locations leading to data inconsistency and loss of information.

Difficulty Monitoring Case Statuses

Inability to efficiently track case progress due to reliance on manual monitoring processes.

Challenges in Managing Evidence

Difficulty managing supporting evidence and records without a centralized digital platform.

Time-Consuming Report Generation

Reviewing large volumes of accident records manually affects the efficiency of generating reports and documentation.

Difficulty Producing Statistics

Difficulty producing accident statistics and summaries from years of manual records for decision-making.

Capabilities

Core Features

TraceDrive offers a comprehensive suite of features designed to streamline vehicle accident records management.

Vehicle Plate Number Search

Allows authorized personnel to retrieve accident-related records associated with a vehicle through its plate number.

Vehicle, Owner, and Driver Information Management

Stores and manages information related to vehicles, registered owners, and drivers involved in recorded accidents.

Accident Incident Recording

Allows personnel to record accident-related information, including date and time, location, accident type, incident description, vehicle information, owner information, driver information, and injury and damage details.

Evidence and Document Management

Supports the uploading, storage, and management of accident-related photographs, reports, and supporting documents.

Case Status Tracking

Allows authorized personnel to monitor and update accident case statuses such as Reported, Under Investigation, Ongoing, Resolved, and Closed.

Search and Filtering

Provides efficient retrieval of records through search and filtering functions based on plate number, vehicle information, driver information, accident date, accident type, and case status.

Accident Statistics and Summaries

Displays visual summaries and statistical reports generated from available accident records, including monthly and yearly totals, accident type distribution, location-based records, and case status summaries.

Report Generation

Generates printable and downloadable reports for documentation and record-keeping purposes.

User Authentication and Role-Based Access Control

Implements secure login and access permissions to ensure that only authorized personnel can access and manage records.

Process

System Workflow

A streamlined process from record entry to report generation.

1

Enter Plate Number

Authorized personnel enters vehicle plate number into the system to search for records.

2

Retrieve Records

System retrieves vehicle and owner records from the centralized database.

3

Record Driver Information

Driver information is recorded including details of the actual driver involved in the accident.

4

Record Accident Details

Accident details are recorded including date, time, location, type, description, and injury and damage details.

5

Upload Evidence and Documents

Evidence and supporting documents are uploaded and managed in the system.

6

Track Case Status

Case status is monitored and updated throughout the resolution process.

7

Generate Reports and Statistics

Reports and accident statistics are generated for documentation and record-keeping purposes.
