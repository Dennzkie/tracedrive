About the Project

Project Overview

TraceDrive is a web-based platform designed to help authorized personnel manage and organize vehicle accident-related records efficiently through a centralized digital system.

Centralized Records

All accident-related data stored in one secure, accessible location for efficient management.

Accident Tracking

Real-time monitoring and tracking of accident cases from initial report to resolution.

Vehicle Ownership Monitoring

Comprehensive vehicle and owner information management for quick identification.

Investigation Support

Tools to assist investigators in managing cases, evidence, and generating reports.

The Challenge

Problem Setting

Current accident record management faces numerous challenges that hinder efficient investigation and documentation.

Manual Accident Records

Paper-based documentation leads to errors, loss of data, and inefficient record keeping.

Scattered Documentation

Records spread across multiple offices and filing systems make retrieval difficult.

Delayed Information Retrieval

Time-consuming manual searches slow down investigations and decision-making.

Difficulty Identifying Drivers

Lack of centralized driver records makes identification challenging during investigations.

Inefficient Case Tracking

No systematic way to monitor case progress from report to resolution.

Process Flow

System Workflow

A streamlined process from incident report to case resolution.

1

Enter Vehicle Plate Number

Investigator inputs the vehicle plate number into the system to begin the process.

2

Retrieve Vehicle & Owner Records

System automatically retrieves associated vehicle and owner information.

3

Record Accident Information

Detailed accident information is documented including location, time, and circumstances.

4

Identify Driver Information

Driver details are identified and linked to the accident record.

5

Upload Evidence

Photos, documents, and other evidence are securely uploaded to the case file.

6

Monitor Case Progress

Investigator tracks the case status and updates throughout the investigation.

7

Generate Reports

Comprehensive PDF reports are generated for official documentation and records.
