Overview

Project Concept

TraceDrive is a centralized web-based Vehicle Accident Tracking and Records Management System designed to assist authorized personnel of the Philippine National Police in managing, monitoring, and organizing vehicle accident-related records through a digital platform.

The proposed system aims to improve accident documentation, information retrieval, case monitoring, and record organization by replacing manual and scattered documentation processes with a centralized digital records management system.

TraceDrive allows authorized personnel to:

Search vehicle plate numbers
Manage vehicle and owner records
Record driver information
Encode accident incidents
Upload accident evidence and documents
Monitor case statuses
Search and filter records
Generate reports
View accident analytics and statistical summaries

The system will utilize authorized vehicular accident and incident datasets provided by the Philippine National Police covering a three-year period to support dashboard analytics, statistical visualization, and monitoring of accident-related information.

Centralized Records

Unified database for all vehicle accident-related records accessible by authorized personnel.

Challenge

Problem Setting

Vehicle accident-related records are commonly managed through manual documentation, paper-based files, and scattered records, which may lead to delays in retrieving information, organizing records, and monitoring accident-related cases efficiently.

In some situations, identifying the actual driver involved in an accident becomes difficult when the vehicle is borrowed or used by someone other than the registered owner. Managing supporting evidence and monitoring case progress may also become challenging without a centralized digital platform.

Additionally, analyzing years of accident-related records manually may affect the efficiency of monitoring processes, report generation, and accessibility of information for authorized personnel.

The absence of a centralized records management and monitoring platform may result in:

Inefficient Record Retrieval

Time-consuming process of searching through physical files and paper-based records for specific information.

Disorganized Documentation

Scattered records across multiple locations leading to data inconsistency and loss of information.

Delayed Monitoring

Inability to track case progress in real-time due to reliance on manual monitoring processes.

Manual Processes

Reliance on handwritten records prone to human error, physical deterioration, and slow processing.

Difficult Analytics

Difficulty analyzing accident trends and statistical information from years of manual records.

Capabilities

Core Features

TraceDrive offers a comprehensive suite of features designed to streamline vehicle accident records management.

Vehicle Plate Number Search

Allows authorized personnel to retrieve vehicle and owner information using the vehicle plate number for faster identification and record access.

Vehicle & Owner Information Management

Stores and manages records of registered vehicles and owners in a centralized database.

Driver Information Recording

Allows personnel to record the actual driver involved in the accident when different from the registered owner.

Accident Incident Recording

Enables encoding and documentation of accident-related information such as date, time, location, accident type, and incident descriptions.

Evidence & File Management

Supports uploading and storage of accident-related photographs and supporting documents.

Case Monitoring & Status Tracking

Allows monitoring and updating of accident-related case statuses such as Ongoing, Under Review, Resolved, and Closed.

Advanced Search & Filtering

Provides efficient searching and filtering of records based on plate number, owner information, accident date, and case status.

Dashboard Analytics & Statistical Reports

Displays visual summaries and statistical reports using the provided three-year accident dataset.

User Authentication & Role-Based Access

Implements secure login and access permissions to ensure that only authorized personnel can access and manage records.
